Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2024

1. Data Collection

Our app uses Google AdMob to show advertisements. AdMob may collect certain data automatically, including but not limited to your device's IP address, Advertising ID, and app interaction data.

2. Data Deletion

We do not store any personal user data on our own servers. However, you can manage and delete the data collected by Google AdMob by:

  • Resetting or Deleting your Advertising ID: Go to your Android device settings > Google > Ads > Reset/Delete Advertising ID.
  • Managing Google Account Activity: Visit Google My Activity to manage data linked to your Google account.
